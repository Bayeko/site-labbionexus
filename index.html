About

Lab BioNexus is an independent initiative founded by a biotechnology engineer with hands‑on experience in microbial downstream purification and laboratory automation.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between experimental work and data intelligence by connecting instruments, automation platforms, and digital ecosystems (LIMS, ELN, MES).

We focus on simplicity, compliance, and interoperability — enabling labs to evolve into fully connected, data‑driven environments.

Solutions

Instrument Integration

Connect ÄKTA systems, Tecan liquid handlers, and CE‑SDS analyzers to unified digital workflows. Automate data capture and result processing.
