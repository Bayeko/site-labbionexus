Who we are

About Lab BioNexus

Lab BioNexus is an independent initiative founded by a biotechnology engineer with hands‑on experience in microbial downstream purification and laboratory automation.

Our mission is to bridge the gap between experimental work and data intelligence by connecting instruments, automation platforms, and digital ecosystems (LIMS, ELN, MES).

We focus on simplicity, compliance, and interoperability — enabling labs to evolve into fully connected, data‑driven environments.

Founded by Amir Messadene

Biotechnology engineer specializing in microbial downstream purification, ÄKTA chromatography systems, and laboratory automation. Hands‑on experience bridging wet‑lab operations with digital infrastructure in GxP‑regulated environments.

Based in Switzerland

How it works in practice

Built for real lab workflows

Here’s what Lab BioNexus looks like in three common lab environments.

QC / Analytical Lab

Your CE‑SDS and chromatography data flows directly into your LIMS. Reports auto‑generate from validated templates. Audit trails are captured at every step — no manual transcription.

Instruments → LIMS → Reports

Process Development

Connect your ÄKTA purification runs and Tecan liquid handler directly to your ELN. Compare batches side‑by‑side with structured data instead of scattered spreadsheets.

ÄKTA → ELN → Analytics

GMP Manufacturing

Ensure 21 CFR Part 11 compliance end‑to‑end. Electronic signatures, validated data exchange between MES and LIMS, and full batch traceability — audit‑ready from day one.

MES → LIMS → Compliance
