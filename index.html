Who We Are

About Lab BioNexus

Lab BioNexus is a Swiss startup focused on connecting lab instruments to modern data systems. We bridge the gap between legacy hardware and digital compliance requirements in the pharma and biotech industry.

Our founding team combines expertise in laboratory informatics, regulatory compliance (GxP, 21 CFR Part 11), and cloud engineering to deliver seamless integration solutions.

Based in Fribourg, Switzerland, we work with QC labs, process development teams, and GMP manufacturers across Europe to eliminate manual data handling and strengthen audit readiness.

What We Offer

End-to-end lab integration

From instrument connectivity to regulatory dashboards — one unified platform.

Instrument Integration

Connects to Mettler Toledo, Sartorius, and other RS232/USB instruments via protocol-specific parsers. Custom parsers for additional instruments delivered within 1–2 weeks.

Workflow Automation

Eliminate manual data transfer between instruments and LIMS, ELN, or MES. Automate result reporting and batch record generation with validated workflows.

Regulatory Compliance

Ensure 21 CFR Part 11 and GxP alignment with secure audit trails and validated data exchange. Electronic signatures and full traceability built in.

Advanced Reporting

Centralize process data into interactive dashboards for real-time insights in R&D and QA. Auto-generate validated reports from structured data.

How It Works in Practice

Built for real lab workflows

Here's what Lab BioNexus looks like in three common lab environments.

QC / Analytical Lab

Your balance, pH, and analytical instrument data flows into a centralized platform with full traceability. Reports auto-generate from validated templates. Audit trails capture every measurement at the source.

Instruments → LIMS → Reports

Process Development

Connect your process sensors and analytical instruments directly to your ELN. Compare batches side-by-side with structured, traceable data instead of scattered spreadsheets.

Instruments → Platform → Structured Data

GMP Manufacturing

Ensure 21 CFR Part 11 compliance end-to-end. Electronic signatures, validated data exchange between MES and LIMS, and full traceability from batch record to instrument log.

MES ↔ LIMS ↔ Audit Trail
