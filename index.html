About Labionexus

Founded by Amir Messadene, biotechnology engineer (HES‑SO Valais‑Wallis) with hands‑on experience in microbial downstream purification at Lonza and GMP data integrity at Roche.

Validation approach

Qualification framework

GAMP5 Category 4: Configured Product

The Labionexus platform is validated as a GAMP5 Category 4 Configured Product. The standard layer is qualified by Labionexus with full vendor documentation, ensuring a robust and auditable foundation.

Site-specific IQ/OQ/PQ qualification

Each client deployment includes a site-specific configurable layer with User Requirements Specification (URS), Risk Assessment (RA), Requirements Traceability Matrix (RTM), and full IQ/OQ/PQ protocols tailored to your environment.

Vendor Qualification Package (VQP)

Every deployment includes a complete Vendor Qualification Package for your quality records. Each deployment includes IQ/OQ/PQ qualification delivered with our CSV partner network.
