If you have questions about how we handle your data, contact us at the address above.

2. What data we collect

2.1 Contact form and email

When you use our contact form or email us directly, we collect your name, email address, and the content of your message. We use this data solely to respond to your inquiry.

2.2 Server logs

This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. When you visit, GitHub may collect standard server log data including your IP address, browser type, referring page, and timestamp. This data is processed by GitHub, Inc. under their own privacy policy. We do not have access to individual server logs.

2.3 Cookies

This website does not use cookies or tracking technologies. We do not use analytics services such as Google Analytics. No advertising or remarketing pixels are present on this site.

3. Purpose and legal basis

We process personal data exclusively for the following purposes:

Responding to inquiries — Legal basis: your consent (by voluntarily submitting the form) and our legitimate interest in communicating with prospective customers (Art. 31 nFADP; Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).

Website operation — Legal basis: legitimate interest in providing a functional, secure website.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Data may be shared with the following service providers, acting as processors on our behalf:

GitHub, Inc. (San Francisco, CA, USA) — website hosting. GitHub is certified under the EU–US Data Privacy Framework.

Infomaniak Network SA (Geneva, Switzerland) — email hosting and domain registration. Data remains in Switzerland.

We do not transfer data to other third parties unless required by law.

5. International data transfers

Server log data processed by GitHub may be stored in the United States. GitHub participates in the EU–US Data Privacy Framework, providing an adequate level of protection as recognised by the Swiss Federal Data Protection and Information Commissioner (FDPIC). Email data is hosted by Infomaniak in Switzerland and does not leave Swiss jurisdiction.

6. Data retention

Contact form submissions and related correspondence are retained for up to 24 months after the last communication, then deleted. Server logs are managed and retained by GitHub according to their data retention policies.

7. Your rights

Under the nFADP and, where applicable, the GDPR, you have the right to:

— Request information about the personal data we hold about you

— Request correction of inaccurate data

— Request deletion of your data

— Object to or restrict processing

— Request data portability

— Withdraw consent at any time (without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing)

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@labionexus.ch. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the FDPIC or your local supervisory authority.

8. Security

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or misuse. All communication with this website is encrypted via TLS (HTTPS).

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
